Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked
Questions

We want your experience to be as seamless and enjoyable as possible. Here are answers to our most common questions. Still have something on your mind? Just ask — we're always happy to chat.

◇
Do I need an appointment?
Yes — we are a private, one-on-one studio by appointment only. No walk-ins. Book online through our website to see available times.
◇
How do I prepare?
Eat a good meal before your appointment and stay hydrated. Wear comfortable clothing with easy ear access, and arrive with clean ears. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior.
◇
Can I bring a friend or family member?
Absolutely! You're welcome to bring up to two support people to your appointment. For larger groups, ask us about a private piercing party.
◇
Does it hurt?
A little — but it's very brief. Most clients describe it as a quick pinch that lasts less than a second. The anticipation is almost always worse than the actual piercing.
◇
Do you pierce children and infants?
Yes! We welcome clients of all ages, including infants 6 months and older. Infants must have received their first DTaP vaccination — please bring proof. A parent or guardian must be present for all clients under 18.
◇
What jewelry do you use?
We use only body-safe materials: implant-grade titanium (nickel-free), solid 14K gold, and medical-grade stainless steel. No mystery alloys, plated jewelry, or acrylic — ever.
◇
How long does healing take?
With our medical-grade piercing device and provided ear care solution, most piercings heal within 6 weeks — much faster than traditional methods. Follow your aftercare consistently and you'll be ready for a jewelry change at 6 weeks.
◇
What aftercare do you recommend?
Clean with your provided ear care solution 3× daily — gently rotate the jewelry 3 times at each cleaning and let air dry. Avoid touching with unwashed hands, alcohol or peroxide, and no swimming for 4 weeks.
◇
What is your cancellation policy?
We ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations and no-shows may be subject to a fee. Life happens — just let us know as soon as you can.
◇
Can I bring my own jewelry?
We only pierce with jewelry from our own curated collection — no exceptions. This ensures the metal quality, sizing, and style are right for a fresh piercing. Once fully healed, we're happy to swap to a piece of your own.
