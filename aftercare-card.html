After Your Appointment

Piercing
Aftercare

Follow these simple steps and your piercing will heal beautifully.

◇  Do This
◇Rinse with sterile saline wound wash twice daily
◇Let the piercing fully air dry after cleaning
◇Sleep on a clean pillowcase — silk is ideal
◇Keep hair products, perfume & makeup away
◇Contact us if anything seems off
✗  Avoid
◇Touching with unwashed hands
◇Rotating or twisting the jewelry
◇Alcohol, peroxide, or tea tree oil
◇Pools, hot tubs & open water for at least 8 weeks
◇Changing jewelry before fully healed

Full healing takes 6–9 months — even when it feels fine sooner.
