Our Method

Safe, Gentle & Sterile — Every Time.

Gentle, hand-pressured, and fully enclosed for your safety.

Our medical-grade piercing device — a quiet, smooth, 3-step process that's safe, gentle, and virtually painless. Not a spring-loaded gun. Not a needle. Just precise, intentional placement designed with your comfort in mind.

◇

Fully Sterile. Every Time.

The jewelry arrives pre-loaded inside a sealed, single-use sterile cartridge. It goes directly from that sealed cartridge into your ear — with zero transfers, zero handling, zero contamination risk. Once used, the cartridge is discarded. No reuse, ever.

◇

Precision in Your Piercer's Hands

Unlike spring-loaded guns that fire with uncontrolled force, our device is hand-pressurized — meaning the speed, angle, and pressure are entirely in Holly's control at every moment. The result is a precise, intentional placement every single time.

◇

The Right Tool for the Job

Hollow needles are designed for cartilage and body piercings — they're the right tool for those placements. For soft earlobe tissue, a medical piercing device is the ideal instrument: one clean, precise motion, no excess tissue trauma, and a faster overall process.

Traditional Piercing Gun

  • ✗ Spring-loaded, uncontrolled force
  • ✗ Plastic parts cannot be fully sterilized
  • ✗ Jewelry handled before entering the ear
  • ✗ Blunt force through tissue causes more trauma
  • ✗ Higher risk of misalignment

Our Medical Piercing Device

  • ✓ Hand-pressurized — full piercer control
  • ✓ Single-use sealed sterile cartridge
  • ✓ Zero transfers — jewelry never touched before your ear
  • ✓ One precise motion — minimal tissue trauma
  • ✓ Safe for all ages — children 5 and older welcome
Our Jewelry

Only the Good Stuff

We don't cut corners on materials. Every piece we pierce with is high-quality and body-safe — chosen because it's what your body deserves.

◇

Implant-Grade Titanium

Nickel-free, hypoallergenic, and lightweight — available in a full range of beautiful colors. Ideal for sensitive skin and fresh piercings.

◇

Solid 14K Gold

Timeless, beautiful, and safe for all skin types. Our 14K solid gold pieces are investment pieces meant to live on your ear for life.

◇

Medical-Grade Stainless Steel

Trusted, durable, and body-safe. Our stainless steel meets the most stringent global standards for nickel release requirements — a reliable choice for most clients.

All jewelry is sourced from vendors we trust, with material certifications to back it up. We do not use mystery alloys, plated metals, or acrylic. If you have known metal sensitivities, let us know at your consultation and we'll select the best material for your body.
