About Emerald & Opal

A boutique ear piercing studio rooted in artistry, intention, and a genuine love for helping people shine.

Meet Your Piercer

Hi, I'm Holly

I'm the founder of Emerald & Opal Piercing Studio — where every ear becomes a work of art. From tiny first studs to golden statement stacks, I work with people of all ages to celebrate their individuality.

My passion lies in helping clients feel confident, radiant, and seen — one beautifully placed piercing at a time. Ear styling is more than aesthetics; it's joy, self-expression, and a little sparkle that lasts.

Come visit and let's create something truly special together.

"Every ear tells a story. I'm here to help you write yours."

— Holly

How We Work

The Emerald & Opal Difference

◇

Safety First, Always

We use only implant-grade titanium, solid gold, and medical-grade stainless steel jewelry from trusted vendors. No mystery alloys, ever. Your healing journey starts with the right materials.

◇

Deeply Personal

No two ears — or clients — are the same. Every appointment is a one-on-one consultation. We take time to understand your anatomy, lifestyle, and vision before anything happens.

◇

A Space You'll Love

Our private studio in Bethany Village is spa-like and serene — flooded with natural light and designed to make you feel at ease the moment you walk in.

Find Us

Bethany Village, Portland

Our studio is located in Bethany Village, a vibrant boutique shopping center in NW Portland — a spa-like, serene space with natural light, a curated retail selection, and easy parking right outside.

Every appointment receives our full attention. You'll never feel rushed or overlooked — this is a thoughtful, unhurried experience where the details matter and you leave feeling genuinely taken care of.
