Good to Know

Your Questions,
Answered

◇
Do I need an appointment?
Yes — we are a private, by-appointment-only studio. No walk-ins. Book online to see availability.
◇
Can I bring someone?
Yes! You're welcome to bring up to two support people. For bigger groups, ask about a piercing party.
◇
Can I bring my own jewelry?
We only pierce with jewelry from our own collection to ensure proper metal quality and sizing for a fresh piercing. Once fully healed, we're happy to swap to a piece of your own.
◇
Do you pierce children?
Yes, children ages 5 and older. Parent or guardian must be present with valid ID for all clients under 18.
◇
What jewelry do you use?
Implant-grade titanium, solid 14K gold, and medical-grade stainless steel. No mystery alloys, ever.
◇
How do I care for it?
Clean with your provided ear care solution 3× daily — rotate jewelry 3 times per cleaning, let air dry. No swimming for 4 weeks. Most piercings ready for jewelry change at 6 weeks.
